Welcome to the tilt-o-meter app.

  • Want to know your level of tilt when playing LOL?
  • Wanna see if your friend is tilted because of a cold streak?
  • Can you bring the tilt-o-meter to zero before than your friends?
  • Play LoL at your best and bring the meter to its lowest point!

tilt-o-meter is a satirical, personal, project with an arbitrary logarithm.

Our goal is to have fun among friends, to try to play at your best each game, and keep the meter as low as possible!

This site does not provide real information other than the last games results.

This application is purely a satire, a joke, a laugh and a tool to compete with your friends.
